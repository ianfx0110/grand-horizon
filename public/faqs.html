Guest Information

Frequently asked questions about your upcoming stay at Grand Horizon Hotel & Resort.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in begins at 2:00 PM. Check-out is expected by 11:00 AM on your day of departure. Early arrivals and late departures can be arranged through our concierge, subject to availability.

Is airport transportation provided?

Yes, we offer complimentary luxury shuttle service for guests staying in our Presidential and Executive suites. For other guests, private transfers can be arranged for a nominal fee.

Are pets allowed at the resort?

While we love all creatures, Grand Horizon maintains a pet-free environment to ensure the comfort and tranquility of all our guests, with the exception of certified service animals.

Do the rooms have high-speed internet?

Absolutely. Every wing of our hotel is equipped with enterprise-grade fiber optic WiFi, available to all guests at no additional charge.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations cancelled more than 48 hours before the arrival date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur a one-night stay charge.

Is there a dress code for the signature restaurants?

We recommend 'Smart Casual' for our evening dining venues. We invite you to embrace the elegance of the evening while remaining comfortable.
